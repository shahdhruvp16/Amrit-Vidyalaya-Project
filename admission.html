Admission

School Brochure Admission Form

Responsive Animated Timeline

1. Admin Office Interaction (5-10 minutes)

Parents are greeted and directed to the admission office. Here, they are asked to fill out an Expression of Interest form. A staff member will then arrange a school tour.

2. Admission Counsellor Interaction (30-40 minutes)

An admission counselor, along with a teacher, will provide detailed information about the school, curriculum, and fees. Required documents will be collected during this interaction.

3. Admission Form (10 minutes)

Parents interested in proceeding will receive an admission form for a fee of Rs 250/-. The completed form and documents must be submitted within 2 days. An interaction with the child will be scheduled for the following Friday.

4. Consent Forms

Parents will need to fill out and submit consent forms for field trips, photo-videography, transport, and an anti-bullying pledge. If the forms are not returned within 3 days, follow-up calls will be made.

5. Student Entrance Test

Students in grade 3 and above will take a small entrance test. If teachers are unavailable, Pinal or Shreya will administer the test.

6. Principal Interaction (5-10 minutes)

Parents and students will have a brief meeting with the Principal. This meeting needs to be scheduled in advance by informing Vipin Sir.

7. Admission Confirmation

The admission team will confirm the admission within 2 working days and inform the parents by phone. Parents will then have 1 week to pay the fees and secure the seat
