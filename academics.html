pre primary

Education at pre primary is not limited to the classroom. Our students enjoy a wealth of learning experiences all over our campus as well as off-campus in the broader community( creative assemblies, excursions, field trips etc.) A walk through pre primary section of the school will insight about hands-on learning activities for preschoolers. From alphabet to math to stories, preschool activities that are engaging and effective. They also participate in an exciting variety of autogenous programs, and competitions and onsite school field trips. The child has one intuitive aim: self development. He desperately wants to develop his resources, his ability to cope with a strange, complex world. He wants to do and see and learn for himself through his senses and not through the eyes of an adult. He becomes a full person. He is educated.”

primary

Students of primary section demand flexibility, resourcefulness, sensitivity from teachers. Our class room experts know how to give students choice and voice to show ways to design personal learning experience. The teacher becomes a participant and co-learner in discussion, asking questions, develop high order thinking in students and perhaps correcting misconceptions. For learning with deep understanding of content teacher stays on toes. No examinations upto class 5. Teaching without textbooks – students learn to learn from engaging with the world. Dual skill based evaluation system to assess the students’ performance. Activity based learning.

secondary

A personalized, skill based , competency-based, learning takes place in secondary section. Why, how, and what questions shape the learning, which are usually designed based on student interests, to blend of individual and teamwork

higher secondary

Amrit Vidyalaya offers 10+2 programs in Science and commerce streams. It strongly fosters a scientific temper within the bonds of humanism; to help the student to become a meaningful part of his environment and to see that courage and industry have their due regard. Amrit treats the commerce stream in a unique discipline with a firm standing of its own. Instead of being considered as one of the various dimensions of Economics, at Amrit, it is to be treated as a subject of study having its own features resulting in enormous scope and applications. It has also a variety of social functions to serve. It transmits the spirit and trends of this ‘Science’ with a social orientation and can develop the related understandings, attitudes, values and skills among the students. Students develop talents and skills in a safe and equitable environment. The school community works diligently to maintain a tradition of excellence in academics and leadership
